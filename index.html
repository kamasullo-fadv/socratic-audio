What's Included in Every Mix

Re-amping (if needed)

If your guitars or bass sound weak, we'll re-amp through one of our amps or premium amp sims to nail the tone.

Dynamics Processing

Punch, energy, and consistency — we level and shape transients without killing dynamics.

EQ & Tonal Balance

Tight low end, clean mids, sparkling highs. Every element sits where it belongs.

Harmonic Enrichment

Analog-style saturation adds warmth, grit, character, and excitement.

Stereo Enhancement

Width, space, and clarity — every instrument has its place in the mix.

Effects & Atmosphere

From subtle reverb to creative delays and modulation, we add space and character as needed.

Minor Editing

Small timing or pitch fixes are included free. Full vocal tuning or quantizing available as add-ons.

Unlimited Revisions

We refine until you're happy. Period.
